--- layout: null section-type: team title: Team --- ## RBOR Team

The RBOR team met at University of Michigan's math PhD program. In 2012, we started designing a puzzle game about monsters that grew from beans, to teach ourselves programming (and distract ourselves from writing our theses). We are:

* Nic Ford - design, development * Amanda Visconti - design, website * Michael Von Korff - design, playtesting * Jordan Watkins - design, development, sound, art * Mary Wootters - design, art
